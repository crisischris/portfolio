about me

Hi, my name is Chris.



Truthfully, my first passion in life was architecture. I’ve spent the last several years working as an architect at the largest architectural firm in the world. During that time I discovered the power of storytelling using VR and AR which snowballed into learning and harnessing game engines and, eventually, programming. I’m working towards parlaying my existing knowledge of architecture, space and human scale and my accumulating knowledge of programming into a more focused career within spacial computing and graphics.

Currently, I work as an architect in Seattle. Aside from being an architect, I am also the point person in the Seattle office for XR duties such as helping to integrate XR into our design workflow and to evangelize new and emerging tool sets / workflows. I see spacial computing as the future, and am very much all-in to help make that a reality. Oh, I am also enrolled in Oregon State’s BS in Computer Science program - which leaves me quite busy.
